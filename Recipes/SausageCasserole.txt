Sausage Casserole
Serves: 4
Time:
Ingredients:
8 Pork Sausages
1 Onion, sliced
2 Garlic Cloves, finely chopped
3 Baking potatoes, chopped into 4cm chunks
4 Carrots, chopped into 4cm chuncks
1tbsp tomato puree
1tbsp Plain Flour
1l chicken stock (made from stock cubes)
1tbsp ready-made English Mustard
Optional: 400g tin butter beans, drained
Optional: Fresh parsley, chopped
Instructions:
Heat a pan on high heat and add some oil, then add the sausages. Brown them all over, then take them out and put them to onw side. It doesn't mater if they aren't cooked all the way through.
Turn down the heat to medium, add the onion and allow it to soften for 5 minutes while constantly moving it around the pan.
Add the garlic, potatoes and carrots and stir for 2 minutes. Then add the tomato puree and stir through.
Add the flour and mix it in thoroughly, ensuring that no big lumps remain.
Cover everything in the stock, adding the mustard and giving it another stir.
Put a lid on the pan and let it simmer for 20 minutes, or until the potatoes and carrots begin to soften.
Return the sausages to the pan (with the optional butter beans). Bring the casserole back to the boil and let it bubble away for a further 7-8 minutes on a low heat.
Stir through the parsley and serve.
