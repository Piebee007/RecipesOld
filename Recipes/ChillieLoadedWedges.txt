Chillie Loaded wedges
Serves: 4
Time:
Ingredients:
500g Beef mince
1 beef stock cube
2 cloves of garlic
2 Bell peppers
900g Potatoes
Mexican spice (Paprika, chillie powder, oregano, mixed herbs)
400g Tomato Passata
120g Cheddar Cheese
1 Tin Black beans
2 Carrots
1 Onion
300ml water
Instructions:
Preheat the oven to 200â„ƒ
Chop the potatoes into 2cm wedges and put on a large roasting tray. Drizzle with oil and season with salt and peppers
Cook until golden and crispy (25-30 minutes). Turn halfway through cooking.
Meanwhile, cut up the pepper and onion. Trim, then coarsely grate the carrot and also the garlic
Heat a splash of oil in a pan over medium-high heat.
When hot add the beef mince and cook until browned.
Add the carrot, pepper, onion, garlic and the mexican seasoning to the pan and cook for a couple of minutes
Add the tomato pssata, water and the beef stock. Stir and bring to the boil
Drain the black beans and give them a rinse. Then add to the pan
Simmer until the sauce is thick (10-12 minutes) stirring occasionally
Whilst the sauce is simmering, grate the cheddar cheese and optionally cut up some spring onions
When the wedges are ready, transfer to an ovenproof dish and put the grill on high. When the chillie is thick, spoon ontop of the wedges and sprinkle over the cheese. Grill until cheese is golden and bubbly
(Optional) Finish with a sprinkle of spring onion
