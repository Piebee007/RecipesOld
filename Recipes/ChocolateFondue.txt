Chocolate Fondue
Serves: 4
Time: 20 minutes
Ingredients:
50g Butter
50g plain chocolate, broken into small chunks
1tbsp cocoa powder
2tbsp Golden Syrup
Instructions:
Place the butter, chocolate, cocoa powder and golden syrup in a small saucepan. 
Heat gently on a hob, stirring constantly, until all of the ingredients have melted and are well combined.
Serve with an assortment of fruit to dip.
