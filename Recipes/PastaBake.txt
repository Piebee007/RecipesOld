Pasta Bake
Serves: 3
Time:
Ingredients:
250g Rigatoni Pasta
1 Jar Tomato and Pepperoni Pasta Bake Sauce
1 Pack of German Salami
2 Balls of Mozzarella
100g Grated Cheddar Cheese
2-3 Cloves of garlic
1 tbsp Balsamic Vinegar
1 tsp mixed herbs
1 tsp Oregano
Instructions:
Cut up 3/4 of the salami into quaters and finely chop the garlic. Leave the rest to one side
Heat a pan to medium heat then add the salami and garlic
Preheat the oven to 180Â°C. Then add the pasta and sauce to a dish. Fill up the jar up to the label with water and pour into the dish
Once the salami has browned, add it to the dish along with the balsamic vinegar, mixed herbs and oregano. 
Mix everything through then put in the oven for 20 minutes. Whilst it is in the oven, grate the cheese and cut the Mozzarella into slices
After 20 minutes, add half of the Mozzarella and all of the grated cheese. Return to the oven for 10 minutes
Place the left over salami on the top of the pasta bake and then add the Mozzarella. Cook for a further 10 minutes until the cheese is melted
