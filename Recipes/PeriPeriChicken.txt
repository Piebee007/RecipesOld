Peri Peri Chicken
Serves:
Time: 2 hours 20 minutes
Ingredients:
2kg whole chicken
5 Garlic cloves
1-5 red chillies (as hot as you dare!)
2cm piece of fresh ginger
50ml vegetable oil
25ml white or red wine vinegar
3 tbsp soy sauce
1 tbsp Tobasco sauce
2 tbsp smoked paprika
3 tsp dried oregano
1 tsp caster sugar
1 tbsp lemon juice
3 red onions
500g baby new potatoes
4 corn on the cob
(Optional) Coleslaw
Instructions:
Preheat the oven to 180Â°C. Sit the chicken in a large roasting tin and season with salt, pepper and a drizzle of oil
Place in the oven for 1 hour. Meanwhile prepare the peri sauce.
For the peri peri sauce, add the following to an electric blender: garlic, chillis, ginger, vegetable oil, white/red wine vinegar, soy sauce, tobasco sauce, smoked paprika, dried oregano, caster sugar and lemon juice
Remove the chicken after 1 hour and tip threequaters of the peri peri sauce onto it, spreading all over with a spoon
Peel and quater the onions and toss them around the chicken along with the potatoes
Return to the oven for another 50 minutes. It will mosy likely look burnt but don't worry about it
Take the chicken out to carve and return the tray to the oven to keep warm
Add the left over peri peri sauce to the chicken
Serve with corn on the cob and optionally coleslaw.
