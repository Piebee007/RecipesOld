Chicken Fajitas
Serves: 4
Time: 45 minutes
Ingredients:
4 Chicken breasts
1 Pepper 
1 Onion
6 Wraps
1 Fajitas Seasoning Packet
1 tsp Lemon Juice
Lettuce
Cucumber
Tomatoe
Cheese 
Instructions:
Cut up the chicken into chunks. Cover them with a bit of oil, lemon juice and the seasoning mixed
(Optional) Marinate the chicken for a few hours
FINISH THIS SECTION
In the mean time, cut up the lettuce, cucumber and tomatoe. Also grate the cheese. Set aside for serving

Cut the onion and pepper up
