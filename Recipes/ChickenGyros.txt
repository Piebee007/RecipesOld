Chicken Gyros
Serves: 4
Time:
Ingredients:
1kg chicken legs
500ml Natural Yogurt
1 Red onion
2-3 Baking potatoes
1 cucumber
250g Self raising flour
2 large tomatoes
1 lemon
1 garlic Bulb
1 packet of roast veg mix
Instructions:
Marinate the chicken legs with lemon juice, yogurt, 3 cloves of garlic and the seasoning mix
When ready to cook, preheat the oven to 180Â°C and cook for 40 minutes
In the mean time, cut up the baking potatoes so they are thin like fries. Cover with oil, salt and pepper
Cook for 20-30 minutes
For the tzatziki, grate up the cucumber and squeeze out the water. Add some yogurt, lemon juice, salt, paper, grated garlic, some olive oil and optionally some dill
For the flatbread, add equal parts self raising flour and yogurt and a pinch of salt
Kneed on an oiled board. Then roll out and cook on a screaching hot pan for a couple of minutes on each side
Shread all the chicken up.
Add to the flatbread: tzatziki sauce, chicken, fries, onion and tomato
