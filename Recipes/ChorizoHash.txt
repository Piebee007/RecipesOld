Chorizo Hash
Serves: 2
Time: 
Ingredients:
120g Chorizo Slices, cut into thin strips
4 Baking Potatoes, cut into litle chunks
4 Medium Eggs
1 Red Chilli, diced
1 Onion, diced
1-2 Spring Onion, sliced
Optional: Red Pepper, diced
Instructions:
Fill a pot with water and bring it to a boil. Add the potatoes and parboil them for 5-10 minutes until they are partially cooked.
Heat a pan over medium heat and add the chorizo. Cook for about a minute, then set it aside.
In the same pan, add the potatoes. Cook until they start to brown and become tender.
Add the diced onion, chili, and optional red pepper to the pan. Cook for 5 minutes until the vegetables soften.
Season the hash with salt and pepper according to your taste. Return the chorizo to the pan.
Set the hash aside and quickly clean the pan. Fry the eggs in the cleaned pan.
To serve, place the fried eggs on top of the chorizo hash and sprinkle with sliced spring onions.
