Beer Can Chicken
Serves:5
Time:
Ingredients:
1 Large whole chicken
2 Cans of light beer
2 cloves of garlic
1 tbsp garlic powder
1 tbsp dried oregano
1/2 tbsp mixed herbs
1 tbsp salt
1 tbsp ground black pepper
1 red onion
Potatoes
Carrots
Frozen peas
Grean Beans
Instructions:
Preheat oven to 175Â°C
In a bowl, mix the garlix powder, dried oregano, mixed herbs, salt, pepper and chopped garlic
Pour 1/3 of a can of beer into the bottom of a baking dish. Place the beer can in the centre of the baking dish
Fit the whole chicken over the open beer can with the legs on the bottom. With the chicken breasts facing you, use a knife to ut a small slit on each side. Press the tip of each wing into the slit
Rub the seasoning mix over the entire chicken
Open the second can of beer and pour 1/2 of it into the pan
Bake the chicken in the preheated oven for 45 minutes. 
Pour the remaining beer into the pan under the chicken and continue to cook until no longer pink and the juices run clear
Once cooked, leave to rest for 10 minutes
25 minutes before the chicken is cooked, get some peeled and chopped potatoes in a pan of cold water and boil
In another pan get some cold water for the carrots, green beans and peas and get boiling.
When boiling add the carrots. Then after 10 minutes add the green beans. With 5 minutes left add the frozen peas
Then serve with some gravy
