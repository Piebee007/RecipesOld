Chicken & Chorizo Pasta
Serves: 2
Time: 20 minutes
Ingredients:
150g Pasta (Rigatoni)
50g Diced Chorizo
2 Cubed Chicken Breasts
1 Diced Onions
2 chopped garlic cloves
Sprinkle of chillie flakes
1tsp Paprika
2tbsp Tomato Puree
100ml Single Cream
4 Handfulls of Spinach
20g Grated Parmesan
Instructions:
Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the pasta and cook according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
Heat a large pan over medium head and add the chicken, chorizo, onion and spices.
Once the chicken is cooked, add the tomato puree and the single cream.
Then add the pasta to the pan, along with the spinach and parmesan.
