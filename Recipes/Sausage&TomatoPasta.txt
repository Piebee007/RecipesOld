Sausage and Tomato Pasta
Serves:4
Time:
Ingredients:
1 pack o good quality sausages (about 400g)
1 tin of plumb tomatoes (400g)
1 large onion, finely chopped
3 garlic cloves, minced
A handful of fresh basil leaves, chopped
2 tbsp cream cheese
400g pasta (penne or fusilli)
Grated Parmesan cheese, to serve
Instructions:
Remove the sausage skins and break up the sausage meat into a hot pan with a bit of oil.
Once the sausage meat starts to brown, add the chopped onions and cook for 2-3 minutes.
Add the chopped garlic and cook for 1 minute.
Pour in the entire can of plum tomatoes and break them up with a spoon.
Fill the empty tomato can halfway with water and add it to the pan. Season with salt and pepper.
In a separate pot, bring salted water to a boil and cook the pasta according to package instructions.
When the pasta is almost done, add the fresh basil and a heaped tablespoon of cream cheese to the sausage and tomato sauce and stir.
Drain the cooked pasta and add it to the pan with the sauce. Mix everything together and serve.
