Jenson's Famous Curry
Serves:4
Time: 30 minutes
Ingredients:
4 Chicken Breasts
1 Jar of Blue Dragon Thair Red Curry Paste
1 chicken stock
4 nests of thin noodles
Chillies
1 tin of coconut milk
Tumeric
Cumin
Honey 
Fish sauce
Instructions:
Heat up frying pan
Heat curry paste for a few minutes with cumin, tumeric and oil
Add chicken and cook till cooked
Add coconut milk, chicken stock, chillies, fish sauce and Honey
Simmer for x minutes
Whilst simmering, cook noodles.
When the noodles are cooked, add to curry
Serve with some chopped chillies for garnish
Tags: Chicken, Curry, Asian
