Chicken ChickenBurgers
Serves: 2
Time: 20 minutes
Ingredients:
2 chicken Breasts
BBQ seasoning
Burger buns
Chips
(Optional) Mozzarella
(Optional) Lettuce
Instructions:
Preheat the oven to 180Â°C and put the chips in
Butterfly the chicken breasts and then add a light coasting of oil.
Then coat in a generous amount of BBQ seasoning
Cook the chicken in a frying pan at a medium heat until cooked. (Optional) use a food temperature probe
(Optional) Add the mozzarella slices to the chicken and serve when melted
Serve on buns with lettuce along with the chips and any other sides
