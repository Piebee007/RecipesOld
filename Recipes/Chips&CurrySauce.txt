Chips & Curry saucepan
Serves: 2
Time:
Ingredients:
1 tin Coconut Milk
1 tbsp Plain Flour
1 chicken stock cube 
1 carrot
1 Onion
1 small knob of ginger
3 cloves of garlic
2 tbsp mild curry powder
2 tbsp soy sauce
4 Jacket Potatoes
Instructions:
Preheat the oven at 180Â°C
Cut up the jacket potatoes into batons (with the skins on). Put into a bowl and drizzle on some oil. Add salt, pepper and a table spoon of curry powder
Put into the oven and cook for 30-40 minutes
For the sauce, add grated carrot and chopped onion to a pan at medium Heat
Let it soften for 3-4 minutes. Then add the garlic and a bit of ginger
After a minute, add a heaped table spoon of curry powder and flour, then mix through
Gradually add chicken stock, mixing everytime. 
Add half a tin of coconut milk and the soy sauce. Stir it through and heat through for 10 minutes
Pass it through a siev or blend
(Optional) For serving add cheese to the chips for the last couple minutes. Garnish with spring onion
